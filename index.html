Our Story

A family shop built on trust & freshness

FreshMart started as a small corner store in Pitampura. Over 12 years, we've grown into a beloved neighbourhood grocery — but our values stay the same: source the best, price it fairly, and treat every customer like family.

We work directly with local farmers and trusted suppliers so you get produce that's picked at peak freshness — never sitting in a warehouse for weeks.
